HowToPledge

Page history last edited by Luther Goh 6 years, 12 months ago

It is no longer necessary to pledge

 

The pledging process was set up to allow us to get together a large enough group of people willing to pay that we could go ahead and sign a lease. Hackerspace.SG has been up and running since November 2009, pledging is no longer necessary.

 

Please do not request access to the wiki's "workspace" simply in order to become a member of Hackerspace.SG.

 

OK, so how do I join?

 

First up, you are welcome (encouraged!) to spend some time a Hackerspace.SG as a guest at any of the public events on the calendar to meet the people and get a feel for the place.

 

In order to join you will need to:

  • Complete a membership form available at Hackerspace.SG
  • Download a form here http://hackerspacesg.pbworks.com/w/page/56106951/Membership%20Form 
  • Make payment as described below.

 

Please direct all membership enquiries by email to membership [at] hackerspace [dot] sg

 

What do I get?

 

Hackerspace.SG's membership tiers are described at  http://hackerspace.sg/membership/

 

How do I pay?

 

Paying by Bank Transfer

 

Perform a bank transfer to the Standard Chartered account with the following details:

Bank code: 7144

Branch code: 057

Standard Chartered Account: 5701304090

Name: Hackerspace.SG Pte. Ltd.

 

For the first membership transfer, pls include an additional month of fees as the membership deposit.

 

Please follow up with an email to membership [at] hackerspace [dot] sg with the subject "Hackerspace Pledge" with your name and transaction details immediately following a transfer, thanks.

 

Bank account details will also be put up on hackerspace.sg as an authoritative source soon.

 

You are strongly encouraged to setup a standing instruction so that membership fund transfers takes place automatically. 

 

 

Payment by Paypal

 

You may use paypal to pay your membership subscriptions. Please note that fees levied by Paypal will be absorbed by you. Paypal details are listed on our website.

 

 

Paying by cheque 

 

Pass the cheque to one of: Meng, Ruiwen, Juanita, Justin or Luther in the Hackerspace and we'll process it.

 

Cheques should be made out to Hackerspace.SG Pte Ltd.

 

Paying by GIRO

 

If you'd like to set up regular payments to HackerspaceSG via GIRO, download this form, and send it to

 

Standard Chartered @ Changi

Account services

7 Changi Business Park Crescent 

Singapore 486028
