Ninja Volunteers

We are always looking for people to help/volunteer. If you would like to help, do drop a mail to our google groups.

 

Here's the list of job descriptions for the our ninja warriors:

 

  • Aircon ninja: Make sure contract is renewed every year, company cleans every quarter, notify contractor if leak exists - aircon has just been cleaned on the 3rd Oct, chemical and condenser cleaning not included. Complaint of aircon leaking. Additional contract changes: 6 mthly condenser cleaning, 3 mthly chemical cleaning. Current items that need to be followed up: Get additional signature on cheque and mail it to them. Our current contractor is Aircon Designs Pte Ltd.

 

  • Insurance ninja: ensure that HSG is covered by building insurance. Just got renewed last month with Justin Ng (xjieke AT gmail DOTT com ). To consider a different provider at next renewal.

 

  • Fire extiguisher ninja: Make sure that fire extinguishers are checked once past expiry, every 2 years. Juanita: Safi Equipment and Services Pte Ltd, Blk 1001 #01-04/20 Eunos Avenue 8, Singapore 409496, Tel: 6745 5151 - $15/extinguisher/year (approx), $15/firehose reel/year + $3.50/kg for CO2 top up if required. An alternative is Firetronics.

 

Chris is the aircon, fire extinguisher and insurance ninja!

 

  • Landlord POC ninja: Liaise with the landlord with regards to rental, infrastructure issues etc. Contact at 6333 6852.

 

Luther is currently the Landlord POC ninja! 

 

  • Door Ninja: Issue new PIN codes in batches and be in charge of issues related to the keypad or magnetic door lock. Remove PIN codes of people who are non longer members, responsible for ensuring all access systems work and sufficient PINs and physical keys are available to membership ninja. 

 

Michael Cheng is the door ninja!

Fazli is the key ninja! 

 

  • Shopping ninja: buy groceries, alcohol and soft drinks every 2 weeks, or whenever required.

 

Aaron is the shopping ninja!

 

  • Namecard ninja: help members generate new namecard templates:  send out the template to new members when requested
  • Email addresses ninja: manage the @hackerspace.sg email addresses

 

CJ is the namecard and email addresses ninja 

 

  • Hackdo ninja: do not deprecate but research if data can be extracted from new accounting software and upload to hackdo.

 

Loong Jin! 

 

  • Arrears ninja: chase members to pay their arrears. There is a Trello board for this.

 

Consist of Luther, Roland, James.

 

  • Wordpress security update ninja: make sure wordpress install always up to date.

PREETAM and CJ

 

  • Wordpress content update ninja: eg Update hsg contact page with the hsg phone number 

 

PREETAM and CJ

 

  • Wordpress blogging team/feed manager: process and update blog feeds 

 

PREETAM 

 

  • Cleaning ninja: Make sure that the space is open when she wants to come and clean. Notify the cleaner when she misses any surface. Have a list of things for her to clean/check against/write last date of cleaning.  

 

Up for grabs!
