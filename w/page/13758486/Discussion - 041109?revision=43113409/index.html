Actions / Points:

  • HackerspaceSG Working Calendar created

    • The HackerspaceSG Working Calendar shows deadlines and milestones for the Hackerspace
    • Currently, events marked on the calendar indicate either deadlines for completion or timespan within which that task should be completed.
    • http://www.google.com/calendar/hosted/hackerspace.sg/embed?src=rwchua%40hackerspace.sg&ctz=Asia/Singapore

  • Responsibilities

    • We discussed some roles and positions that could be taken up in the Hackerspace, and here they are below. Great thanks to them for volunteering!

    • Webmaster (Blog)

      • Adrian Oliveiro
      • Tamas Herman

    • Calendar / Events Master

      • Justin Lee
      • Kelvin Ng

    • Membership Master

      • Ruiwen Chua
      • Still open

    • Accountant / Treasurer

      • To handle the financial records for the Hackerspace
      • Still open

    • Operations Manager

      • To be responsible for day-to-day logistics for the Hackerspace
      • Still open

    • Council

      • The community council, to help lead and direct the Hackerspace community

      • Still open

  • On booking of Hackerspace for events

    • Here's the general consensus

      • External parties need to be endorsed by at least one paying Hacker

      • With the endorsement, they can approach the Calendar / Events Masters to book a slot, on a first-come-first-served basis, at least one week in advance.

      • Calendar / Events Masters to mark the requested slot on the public HackerspaceSG Events Calendar (not created yet)

      • Community has lead time of one week to raise violent objects to the Calendar / Event Masters

      • Should number of violent objections reach a Threshold, the Calendar / Event Masters will reject the event.

    • Others

      • Calendar / Events Masters can reserve one event-less day a week to be kept just for hacking

    • To be decided

      • What is the Rejection Threshold?

      • How  are violent objections raised?

      • Should there be a minimum of days before the scheduled date of an event when the event is "golden" and cannot be rejected?

  • On corporate sponsorship

    • (Pending clarification of legal issues surrounding the acceptance of sponsorship)

    • The Hackerspace can accept sponsorship from corporate sponsors

    • The Hackerspace will acknowledge the sponsorship, perhaps through a mention on the blog, or similar.

    • Sponsorship does not confer voting or management rights.
