Vision

A dedicated space for inquisitive minds to innovate, collaborate, experiment and develop (ICED).

 

Purpose

Filling the gap that Singapore is lacking

  • A focal point for such people to meet - there are people, they just don't know each other.

 

Interesting Snippets

  • Be the Zouk of Geekdom
  • Satisfying intellectual hunger
  • Unleashing creativity
  • Being yourself
  • Going where your mind can take you
  • Ignition of minds

 

Company

  • To facilitate the running and management of Hackerspace.
  • A breeding ground for experimentation and innovation.

 

Society vs. Company

  • Company
    • Acts in the interest of the community.
    • Makes no sense to screw the community in that it depends on the community for survival
    • Community vetted business plan on the wiki
  • Society
    • Community supported - volunteers, contributions, etc

 

Audience

  • People with ideas and need a place to hack out a prototype
  • Freelancers who just need a place
  • Students who wants to work on something but needs guidance from others
  • User Groups and Community meetups 

 

Revenue

  • Membership fees
  • Event hosting
  • Referral fees
  • Daytime office rent
  • Working team consulting
    • Be the middle man and take a cut

 

Membership

  • Office space rental - $500/mth
  • $100 / mth for normal membership  
  • $50 / mth for student membership
  • Daily membership?
  • Tiered 1/3/4/6/12 months payment?

 

Infrastructure:

  • Wiki page
    • Booking System
    • List of projects being worked on
  • Events
    • Weekly or Monthly hackfest night
    • Presentation nights
    • Idea nights (lightning talks)
    • User Groups

 

What we are not

  • We are not an incubator.
  • We are not a crime den.
  • We are not an F&B outlet.

 

Going Forward

  • Getting Awareness
    • Presentation Slides
    • Speaking to schools and other groups about this place
  • Sourcing a place
  • Cost Analysis (new page)
  • Raising Funds
