HowToPledge


It is no longer necessary to pledge

 

The pledging process was set up to allow us to get together a large enough group of people willing to pay that we could go ahead and sign a lease. Hackerspace.SG has been up and running since November 2009, pledging is no longer necessary.

 

Please do not request access to the wiki's "workspace" simply in order to become a member of Hackerspace.SG.

 

OK, so how do I join?

 

First up, you are welcome (encouraged!) to spend some time a Hackerspace.SG as a guest at any of the public events on the calendar to meet the people and get a feel for the place.

 

In order to join you will need to:

  • Complete a membership form available at Hackerspace.SG
  • Download a form here /w/page/56106951/Membership%20Form 
  • Make payment as described below.

 

Please direct all membership enquiries by email to membership [at] hackerspace [dot] sg

 

What do I get?

 

Hackerspace.SG's membership tiers are described at  http://hackerspace.sg/membership/
