NOTE:

Currently resolving issues with Pledgie. DO NOT PLEDGE THROUGH PLEDGIE UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE.

 

Further Notice (05 Sept 09):

Looks like a Personal Payment in Paypal is suitable for our needs without additional fees. See "How to Pledge" below, for details

 

Why Pledge?

As noted by Jason on the HackerspaceSG mailing list, we need to raise funds in order to make the Hackerspace a reality.

 

With the amount raised, we will then be able to:

  1. Satisfy basic operational expenses, eg. food+drinks for all, venue rental, communal equipment, etc.
  2. Show possible funding sources that the Hackerspace is in demand, and will do the community good.

 

The target amount has been set to SGD 15,000 initially. While this may seem like a huge sum at first, it may be only barely sufficient to cover the first three months' rental deposit for, say, a shophouse.. without renovation and utility costs. But, of course, baby steps first.

 

What Do I Get?

 

Meng has proposed membership tiers at http://hackerspace.sg/membership/

 

How to Pledge?

Here's how to pledge, in a few easy steps (you will need an active Paypal account)

  1. Visit the <TO BE FILLED>
  2. In the green box below the words "Make A Pledge", enter the amount you wish to pledge to the Hackerspace, and hit the Submit button.
  3. You'll then be taken to a confirmation screen, followed by a button to go to Paypal to complete the transaction.
  4. Once you get confirmation of the transaction, you Pledge should be complete.
  5. The Pledgie Campaign owner (Ruiwen), will be able to see the list of donors and the amount donated. However, this list will not be shown publicly.
  6. You can also choose to follow up on your donation with a short mail to ruiwen[plus]paypalhackerspacesg [at] thoughtmonkeys [dot] com

 

  1. Log into Paypal
  2. Initiate a Personal Payment to ruiwen[plus]paypalhackerspacesg [at] thoughtmonkeys [dot] com (<--usual unmangling applies)

  3. Complete the rest of the transaction as per normal Paypal procedure.
  4. Please follow up with an email to the thoughtmonkeys [dot] com email address above, stating your name, and amount donated, so that we can double check.

 

Important: The premise of this donation drive is that we will take/make use of the money IF, AND ONLY IF, the Hackerspace moves into operation. Otherwise, the funds are to be refunded if the plans fail for whatever reason. Therefore, please do not make your pledges anonymous. (Unless you're feeling extremely generous =)

 

Pledge wisely!
